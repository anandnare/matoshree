About Our Institution

Empowering healthcare leaders through world-class academic knowledge and ethical, hands-on clinical mastery.

Welcome to Matoshree Institute of Paramedical Sciences, Murum

Matoshree Institute of Paramedical Sciences, Murum, popularly known as MIPS in the Education sector is equipped with Advanced Studies Courses and was established in Murum, Omarga with the objective of providing quality Paramedical care education for 10th, 10+2, graduate and post graduate levels.

Young aspirants study in a stimulating environment of independent thinking and innovation in various disciplines of Health Care, Hospitality and self-employment. MIPS is focused on value addition to the courses that it offers to the student community and provides the unique combination of Health excellence, opportunities, choices and experience.

With rapid globalization of all economic activities, it is imperative that students develop a global outlook to become successful in the International arena. The Institution is working for promotion of the Paramedical Health Education in the Country. This Institution is working towards creating a band of well trained Paramedical Professionals.

We believe in creating a supportive and stimulating learning environment where students can thrive and reach their full potential. Our experienced faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and industry partnerships ensure that our graduates are well-prepared to meet the challenges and opportunities of the healthcare field. Join us at Matoshree Institute of Paramedical Sciences and embark on a rewarding journey towards a fulfilling career in paramedical sciences.
